community help

This where a group of people comes together to help a child

  • Community help is where you join a certain group of people to help a student based on what you could offer. It may not necessarily be money, it could be a skill or educational supplies that can be offered by you free of cost or at a low price. 
    Check out the various communities and you will find your demand.

i have only heart

You can donate anything you wish to donate

  • I have only heart is a special type of scheme where you can offer any means of help possible for you. It may be a school supply or a skill that you know or a service you offer professionally for free of cost. 
    It totally depends on you. It's an example that even a small step can make a big difference.
